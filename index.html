About Event Nest NZ

Event Nest NZ was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to make event planning easier by connecting organizers with the best service providers across New Zealand.

Our platform brings together a diverse network of vetted professionals who are passionate about delivering exceptional event experiences. From corporate functions to weddings, cultural celebrations to community gatherings, we help make every event successful.

What sets us apart is our commitment to supporting local businesses and showcasing the rich cultural diversity of Aotearoa New Zealand. We're proud to feature Māori and Pasifika service providers alongside other talented professionals from across the country.

Our Values

These core principles guide everything we do at Event Nest NZ, from how we build our platform to how we interact with our community.

Community Connection

We believe in the power of bringing people together. Our platform is designed to foster meaningful connections between event organizers and service providers, creating a supportive community that celebrates shared success.

Trust & Quality

We're committed to maintaining high standards across our platform. Every service provider undergoes a thorough vetting process to ensure our users can trust in the quality and reliability of the services they book.

Cultural Diversity

We celebrate the rich cultural tapestry of Aotearoa New Zealand. We actively promote and support Māori and Pasifika service providers, ensuring authentic cultural experiences are accessible and respected.

Innovation

We're constantly evolving our platform to better serve our users. By embracing new technologies and responding to feedback, we strive to create the most efficient and user-friendly event service marketplace in New Zealand.

Local Economic Support

We're passionate about supporting local businesses and entrepreneurs. By providing a platform for service providers to showcase their talents, we contribute to the growth and sustainability of New Zealand's event industry.

Accessibility & Inclusivity

We believe everyone should have access to exceptional event services. Our platform is designed to be inclusive and accessible to all users, regardless of event size, budget, or location across New Zealand.
