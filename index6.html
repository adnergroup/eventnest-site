About Event Nest NZ

Based in Porirua, Wellington, Event Nest NZ was founded with a simple mission: to make event planning easier and more accessible for everyone in Aotearoa. We believe in the power of local talent and aim to connect you with the best vendors in your community, ensuring every event is memorable, successful, and uniquely Kiwi.
