Privacy Policy – EventNest NZ

Effective Date: 21 July 2025
Last Updated: 21 July 2025

EventNest NZ ("EventNest", "we", "our", or "us") is a service platform operated by AdnerGroup NZ Limited, a New Zealand-based business. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in accordance with the Privacy Act 2020 (New Zealand).

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you access or use our website (https://eventnest.co.nz), forms, or services.

1. Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information from you directly when you:

  • Submit an enquiry or service request on our platform
  • Sign up as an event organiser or service provider
  • Contact us or interact with us via email, website forms, or social media

The types of personal information we collect may include:

  • Your full name, email address, phone number, and business name (if applicable)
  • Event details and service requirements
  • Communications, feedback, and any data you submit voluntarily
  • IP address, device/browser information, and usage data (via cookies or analytics tools)

2. Purpose for Collecting Information

We collect your personal information to:

  • Enable connection between event organisers and service providers
  • Facilitate quotations, bookings, and other event-related services
  • Respond to your queries and provide support
  • Improve our website and service offerings
  • Send important notices or service-related communications
  • Comply with legal obligations or protect legal interests

We may also use anonymised, aggregated data for research or analytical purposes.

3. Disclosure of Personal Information

We may share your personal information with:

  • Service providers registered on our platform
  • Third-party vendors under confidentiality agreements
  • Our parent company, AdnerGroup NZ, for operations and compliance
  • Government agencies or law enforcement, when legally required

We do not sell or rent your personal information to any third party.

4. Data Storage and Security

All personal information is stored securely using encrypted servers managed by us or reputable third-party providers. These providers may be located in New Zealand or countries with comparable privacy safeguards. We apply industry-standard measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure.

However, no system is 100% secure. Users are responsible for keeping their login credentials confidential.

5. Your Rights Under the Privacy Act 2020

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent to certain uses
  • Lodge a complaint with the NZ Privacy Commissioner

Contact us at admin@adnergroup.co.nz to exercise these rights.

6. Cookies and Website Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Improve your browsing experience
  • Track website performance and usage
  • Enable platform functionality

You can manage cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features.

7. Retention of Personal Information

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for our business purposes or to comply with legal obligations. Data that is no longer required will be securely deleted or anonymised.

8. Links to Other Sites

Our website may include links to third-party websites. These sites operate independently and are not covered by this policy.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will be posted on this page with the updated date above.
